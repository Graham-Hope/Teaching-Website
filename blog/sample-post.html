How to sound natural in small talk

Sample post • 5 min read

Want to sound more natural when chatting in English? Try these steps:

  1. Use short follow‑ups: “Gotcha”, “No way!”, “Nice one”.
  2. Ask easy open questions: “How was your weekend?”
  3. Mirror vocabulary: repeat one or two keywords the other person used.
  4. Keep answers concrete: people, places, numbers, examples.

Practice 10 minutes a day and record yourself. You’ll hear fast progress.
