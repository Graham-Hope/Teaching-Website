Frequently Asked Questions

Where do lessons take place?

Online via Zoom or in Taipei city center (cafés or coworking spaces).

How much do lessons cost?

NT$1,200 per hour for general English. Business/Interview prep NT$1,500 per hour.

Can I take a trial class?

Yes, the first 20 minutes are free. Book a time on the homepage.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please cancel or reschedule at least 12 hours in advance. Late cancellations may be charged.

Do you teach kids?

I primarily teach adults and university students. Teens are okay with parental consent.
